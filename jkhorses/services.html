SERVICES

JK Horses is devoted to providing quality and cost-effective farrier and equine dentistry services throughout the Phoenix Area. He can provide hoof and mouth care for any number of horses at any equine accessible location. His appointments are scheduled in advanced, if he is running late for any reason he will call or text to give you a heads up.

Horse shoeing services include

  • Farrier/Horseshoeing Consultations He would be happy to come out for a consultation for new customers.
  • Aluminum or Steel Shoes Joseph will help you decide what is best for you and the needs of your animal
  • Barefoot Care Natural Hoof Care -Hot Shoeing When its beneficial to the horse, hot shoeing can be used.
  • Therapeutic Shoeing Joseph is a hoof care specialist that helps equine athletes achieve peak performance through therapeutic shoeing
  • Pads & Wedges Various types of pads and wedges for different situations.
  • Consultations on Radiographs/Ultrasounds Willing to work with the veterinarian when needed.

Equine Dentistry Services include

  • Routine Maintenance - removal of sharp points to allow for an even grinding pattern
  • Buckle Points
  • Overbites
  • Wolf Teeth
